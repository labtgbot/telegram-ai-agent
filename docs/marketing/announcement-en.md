**Telegram AI Agent — one bot, every modality, 50 % cheaper.**

We just opened the beta of an AI agent that lives entirely inside
Telegram:

- **Images** via Imagen / FLUX.
- **Short videos** from a single prompt.
- **Chat with GPT, Claude, or Gemini** through a unified token
  economy.
- **Voice transcription** and **document analysis**.
- **Web search** without leaving the chat.

Pricing is in **Telegram Stars** — 250 ⭐ for a 500-token starter
pack, half of what comparable Telegram AI bots charge. New users get
50 tokens on signup and a daily streak bonus.

Why we shipped this:

- Telegram is the right runtime for AI assistants: native push,
  payment rails, and a Mini App SDK that doesn't need a separate
  account.
- The competition (Mira, ChatOn) leaves room on price and on the
  voice / document flows.
- We're documenting the token economy, pricing and architecture
  publicly so anyone can verify the numbers.
