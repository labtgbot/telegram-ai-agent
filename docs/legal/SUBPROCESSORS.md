# Sub-processors

This page lists the third parties that may process personal data on our
behalf under the GDPR. Adding a sub-processor triggers a 30-day notice for
DPA customers (see [`DPA_TEMPLATE.md`](DPA_TEMPLATE.md) §5).

**Last updated:** 2026-05-16.
