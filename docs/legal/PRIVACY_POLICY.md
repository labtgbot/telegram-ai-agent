# Privacy Policy — Telegram AI Agent

**Effective date:** 2026-05-16
**Last updated:** 2026-05-16
**Controller:** labtgbot (the "Operator", "we", "us"). Contact:
[privacy@labtgbot.example](mailto:privacy@labtgbot.example).

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect when you use the
**Telegram AI Agent** bot, its Telegram Mini App and the Admin CRM
(collectively, the "Service"), how we use it, and the rights you have under
the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and equivalent regimes.

If anything below is unclear, please reach out before using the Service.

---

## 1. Scope

This policy covers data processed when you:

- start a chat with the bot,
- open the Mini App from Telegram,
- buy tokens with Telegram Stars,
- interact with AI features (text, image, video, voice, document, search).

It does **not** cover Telegram itself (governed by
[Telegram's Privacy Policy](https://telegram.org/privacy)) or third-party AI
providers we proxy your requests to (Section 5).

## 2. What we collect

| Category | Examples | Source |
|----------|----------|--------|
| Account identifiers | Telegram user ID, username, first/last name, language code | Telegram WebApp `initData` |
| Service state | Token balance, premium status, referral code, claim streak | Generated by the Service |
| Usage data | Prompts, completions, generated media, request metadata, IP address (logs) | Your interaction with the bot/Mini App |
| Payments | Telegram Stars charge IDs, invoice IDs, transaction amounts | Telegram Bot API webhooks |
| Audit data | Admin login attempts, security events | Generated by the Service |

We **do not** ask for, or store, your email, phone number, postal address
or payment card details. Telegram handles payment authorisation; we only
receive a charge identifier we use for reconciliation.

## 3. Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

- **Contract performance (Art. 6 (1) (b))** for account identifiers,
  service state and payment records — without them we cannot deliver the
  Service you asked for.
- **Legitimate interest (Art. 6 (1) (f))** for security logs, abuse
  detection and analytics aggregates. You can object at any time
  ([privacy@labtgbot.example](mailto:privacy@labtgbot.example)).
- **Consent (Art. 6 (1) (a))** for any optional feature that requires it
  (marketing broadcasts, age verification for 18+ content). Consent is
  freely withdrawable.

## 4. Retention

| Category | Retention |
|----------|-----------|
| Active account profile | While your account exists |
| Chat history (free tier) | 24 hours in cache; not persisted |
| Chat history (premium) | 90 days, or until you delete it |
| Generation outputs (images, video) | 30 days |
| Token usage logs | 12 months (aggregated thereafter) |
| Transactions | 6 years (accounting / tax obligation) |
| Server access logs | 14 days |
| Soft-deleted accounts | Anonymised after 30 days (Section 7) |

Backups are encrypted and rotated within 35 days.

## 6. Security

- TLS 1.2+ for all client traffic.
- Telegram WebApp `initData` is verified with HMAC-SHA256 on every request.
- Admin access requires JWT + TOTP (super-admin) and is rate-limited.
- Application and database backups are encrypted at rest.
- Periodic vulnerability scanning is wired into CI
  (`.github/workflows/security.yml`).

See [`docs/SECURITY.md`](../SECURITY.md) for the technical detail.

## 7. Your rights

Under GDPR you can:

- **Access** your data — `GET /api/v1/user/me/export` returns a JSON copy.
- **Rectify** inaccurate data — most fields update automatically from
  Telegram; for anything else, contact us.
- **Erase** your account — `DELETE /api/v1/user/me` schedules deletion in
  **30 days** (grace period). You can cancel by calling
  `POST /api/v1/user/me/cancel-deletion` before the deadline.
- **Restrict** or **object** to processing —
  [privacy@labtgbot.example](mailto:privacy@labtgbot.example).
- **Portability** — the export is machine-readable JSON.
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (e.g. unsubscribe from broadcasts).
- **Lodge a complaint** with your local supervisory authority. EU users:
  see <https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/about-edpb/members_en>.

We respond to requests within 30 days (extendable by another two months
for complex cases — we will tell you if so).

### How erasure works

When you call `DELETE /api/v1/user/me`, we:

1. Record a deletion request with `scheduled_for = now + 30 days`.
2. Keep your account usable so you can cancel within the grace period.
3. After the deadline, our worker
   (`app/workers/account_deletion.py`) anonymises your profile (clears
   name, username, language), removes derived content (chat history,
   generations) and detaches you from referral / login state.
4. Transaction records are **retained** in pseudonymised form to satisfy
   our legal accounting obligation (6 years).

## 8. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 16. If you are between 13
and 16, parental consent is required. We will delete data we learn was
collected from a child without consent.

## 9. International transfers

We may transfer data to providers outside the EEA / UK. Such transfers
rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (Commission Decision 2021/914) plus
supplementary measures (encryption in transit and at rest, pseudonymisation
where possible).

`Last updated` field above tracks revisions.
